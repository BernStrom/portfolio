I created this app as my favortie holiday season was on the horizon and what better way to get into the merry holiday spirits than to build a cafe menu-theme app that serves spice coffee and treats! Now I've set the mood, I've always wanted to have my very own cozy cafe business (and that dream is still alive and well, by the way...) that serves speciality coffee and unique treats and created something that would give me admin rights to access things like inventory management, adding new items to menu, etc., just like an actual business owner would have.

If you would like to try out the admin the inventory features, simply login with either of the three options in the app and you'll have access. To try out the app just like a customer, you can use the following store ID login: fancy-adorable-elves.
