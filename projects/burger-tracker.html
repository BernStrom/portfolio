Where art thou, best burgers? üçî

Sick and tired of having to shuffle through endless restaurants in the city when you just want to narrow it down to a particular search category, say burgers? Well, that's what this app is designed for! You don't even need to key in your location, just simply run the app wherever you are and it will return all the burger joints within the city you're residing in. üòã

My teammates and myself at General Assembly went with this idea due to the on-going Covid-19 pandemic where eating out in Melbourne is huge hassle and the rarity of available dine-in times with restaurants is more precious than diamonds! Instead of an actual booking form with the restaurant, we created a mockup of the booking form as it wasn't legal for us to actually store data being sent back from Google Maps APIs.
